Delete Account

You can delete your account directly from within the app.

Steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the app
  2. Login using your account
  3. Go to Profile or Settings
  4. Tap on "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your request

Data Deletion

Once you delete your account, all your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers within 7 days.

Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes.
